    FAQ

    • How long does the email address live?

      Each inbox is valid for 20 minutes by default and then all messages are purged.

    • Can I extend the expiration?

      Yes—before it expires, click “Create Inbox” again to reset the 20-minute timer on your current ID.

    • Do you store any personal data?

      No personal registration is required. We only store incoming messages and delete them after expiration.

    • Is this service free?

      Absolutely. FlashMail is free to use, no ads and no signup required.

    • Are there any limits on usage?

      You can create as many inboxes as you like. Each inbox can receive unlimited messages within its 20-minute lifetime.
