FAQ

  • How long does the email address live?

    Each inbox is valid for 20 minutes by default and then all messages are purged.

  • Can I extend the expiration?

    Yes—before it expires, click “Create Inbox” again to reset the 20-minute timer.

  • Do you store any personal data?

    No—no registration or personal data is ever collected or stored.

  • Is this service free?

    Yes—fully free, ad-supported, no signup required.

  • Are there any usage limits?

    Unlimited inboxes; each accepts unlimited messages for its 20-minute life.

About Us

FlashMail is a fast, privacy-friendly disposable email service designed to help you protect your real inbox from spam, tracking, and unnecessary clutter. We believe everyone deserves simple tools to stay safe online—without giving up convenience.

Unlike most platforms, we don’t ask for registration, passwords, or personal details. When you click “Create Inbox”, we generate a random address that stays active for 20 minutes. During that time, it can receive messages instantly and anonymously.

Our entire infrastructure is built to be lightweight, serverless, and secure. FlashMail does not use cookies, does not track your activity, and does not store messages beyond their lifespan. It’s free, open by design, and supported by minimal third-party ads.
