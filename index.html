    FAQ

    • How long does the email address live?

      Each inbox is valid for 20 minutes by default and then all messages are purged.

    • Can I extend the expiration?

      Yes—before it expires, click “Create Inbox” again to reset the 20-minute timer on your current ID.

    • Do you store any personal data?

      No personal registration is required. We only store incoming messages and delete them after expiration.

    • Is this service free?

      Absolutely. FlashMail is free to use, no ads and no signup required.

    • Are there any limits on usage?

      You can create as many inboxes as you like. Each inbox can receive unlimited messages within its 20-minute lifetime.

    About Us

    FlashMail is a simple, no-frills disposable email service designed to protect your privacy and keep your real inbox clean. No registration or personal data required—just click “Create Inbox” and start receiving mail instantly. Perfect for newsletter sign-ups, one-time verifications, or any situation where you need a temporary address. Built with modern serverless technology for instant global performance and automatic cleanup.

    Privacy Policy

    Effective Date: May 7, 2025

    1. Introduction

    FlashMail (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a free disposable email service. Your privacy matters—this policy explains what we collect, why, and how we use it.

    2. Data We Collect

    • Email Content: We parse and store incoming message headers and bodies in Cloudflare Workers KV for up to 20 minutes, then they are deleted permanently.
    • Logs & Analytics: We keep minimal request logs (IP, timestamp, User-Agent) for abuse prevention and debugging; logs are rotated and purged regularly.

    3. Use of Data

    We only use your data to deliver messages to your temporary inbox and to maintain service security and performance.

    4. Third-Party Services

    • Mailgun: handles SMTP-to-webhook ingestion of inbound email.
    • Cloudflare Workers & KV: runs our API and stores messages with TTL.
    • GitHub/Cloudflare Pages: serves static assets via CDN.

    5. Data Retention

    Messages expire 20 minutes after arrival; logs are kept for up to 30 days then deleted.

    6. Security

    HTTPS everywhere, strict Content-Security-Policy, and serverless isolation minimize risk.

    7. Children’s Privacy

    Not intended for children under 13; we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

    8. Changes to Policy

    We may update this policy—changes take effect when posted. Continued use implies acceptance.
